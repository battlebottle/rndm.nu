Qouta

Anonymous users have a qouta of 100,000 request a day. There is also a qouta of 10,000,000 numbers a day or 64mb a day. Patreon supporters of any level will receive an API key which increases this to at least 10,000,000 requests a day, 10,000,000,000 numbers a day and 2gb a day respectively.

Requests for less that less than 32 individual numbers do not count towards the number qouta. Requests for 2kb of random bytes do not count towards the raw binary qouta.
